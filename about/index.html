_techsnow story

I’m software developer from Russia. Like every developer I’m reading lot’s of technical articles on english and on russian as well. During tea breaks on my work I had a nice discussions with my collegues about most interesting articles we read. So when I shifted to Singapore I lost ability to discuss russian articles, because most of my collegues don’t know russian. But there is so many awesome tech articles in russian. So I decide to translate most interesting article to english and post it here.
